# Survey Document Formatting Guidelines

Use this document as a reference when preparing files for **Upload Document** in the Surveys admin.

## Recommended structure

- **Use clear section headings** (one per line).  
  Example:
  - `Section: Demographics`
  - `Section: Program Outcomes`

- **Write one question per line** under a section heading.

- **Keep questions explicit and unambiguous**:
  - Prefer complete sentences.
  - Avoid multiple questions in one line.

## Example

Section: Demographics  
What is your role at the organization?  
How long have you been in your current role?

Section: Feedback  
How satisfied are you with the training program?  
What should we improve?

## Tips

- If your document includes bullets or numbering, keep it consistent.
- If the parser struggles, try exporting to `.txt` and using the example structure above.
